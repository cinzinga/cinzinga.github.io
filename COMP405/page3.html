Certifications

In my free time, I enjoy challenging myself to earn industry leading certifications that will make me competitive upon graduation.

OSCP

The Offensive Security Certified Professional (OSCP) is an introductory penetration testing certification. It is considered the gold-standard for junior penetration testers. The exam is a 24-hour hands on exam where students must demonstrate proficiency in the following areas:

  • Network Vulnerability Scanning
  • Privilege Escalation
  • Basic Buffer Overflows

Security+

The Security+ certification by CompTIA is a very introductory cyber security certification. It is a written test consisting of multiple choice questions. This certification teaches the core knowledge needed to move forward in a cyber security career. A student will learn:

  • Secure Design & Implementation
  • Operations & Incident Response
  • Risk & Compliance
